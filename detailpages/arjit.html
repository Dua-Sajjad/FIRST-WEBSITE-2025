Arjit Singh

Arijit Singh is an Indian playback singer, composer, and music producer known for his soulful voice and versatility across various music genres. He was born on April 25, 1987, in Jiaganj, West Bengal, India. Arijit rose to prominence with his hit song "Tum Hi Ho" from the 2013 Bollywood film Aashiqui 2, which became an instant classic. He has sung numerous popular tracks across Hindi, Bengali, and other languages, with hits like "Channa Mereya" (Ae Dil Hai Mushkil), "Raabta" (Agent Vinod), and "Agar Tum Saath Ho" (Tamasha), to name a few. Arijit is celebrated for his emotional depth and ability to connect with listeners through his music. Over the years, he has won several prestigious awards, including Filmfare Awards for Best Playback Singer, and has established himself as one of the most sought-after singers in the Indian music industry. His music spans multiple genres, including romantic ballads, soulful melodies, and peppy tracks. Aside from his playback singing career, Arijit is also a composer and has contributed to the music of several films. His grounded personality and focus on his music have earned him a massive fan following.
